# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Cross-border growth, category, and merchandising teams who need to scan overseas social and ecommerce signals before choosing products, creators, and campaign angles.

## Product Purpose

The product turns a trend category, audience, or use case into a daily overseas hot-product radar. Success means a user can see the strongest social posts, vertical creators, and commercially active opportunities with traceable source links and verifiable fields.

## Brand Personality

Analytical, decisive, evidence-led. The interface should feel like an operating desk for market discovery rather than a marketing landing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS hero pages, vague AI insight cards, one-color purple dashboards, and unsupported claims that imply full real-time coverage where platform access is restricted.

## Design Principles

- Show evidence before conclusions.
- Make freshness, source status, and market coverage visible.
- Keep the workflow dense but scannable.
- Separate platform coverage from data availability.
- Treat every insight as something the user can trace back.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-operable controls, readable dense tables, reduced-motion support, and labels that do not rely on color alone.
